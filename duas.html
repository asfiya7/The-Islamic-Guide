Collection of Duas

Upon Going to Sleep

اللهم بسمك اموت واحيا
Allahumma bismeka amootu wa ahiya
O Allah, with Your Name I die and live.

Upon Waking Up

الْحَمْدُ للهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا وَإِلَيْهِ النُّشُورُ
Alhamdu lil lahillathi ahyaana baada ma amatana wa ilaihin-nushoor
All thanks and praise to Allah Who has given us life after causing us to die (i.e. sleep), and unto Him is the Resurrection.

Upon Completing Ablution

أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَا إِلَهَ إلَّا اللهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ، وَأَشْهَدُ أَنَّ مُحَمَّداً عَبْدُهُ وَرَسُولُهُ
Ashhadu ana la ilaha ilalah wah dawho la sharika lawho wa ashadu ana, Muhammadan abduwho wa rasulhwho
I testify that there is no god but Allah and that Muhammad is the servant of Allah and His Messenger.

Upon Going to the Fajr Prayer

اللَّهمَّ اجْعَلْ فَي قَلْبِي نُوْرا...
Alahumma ijal fi qelbee nuran...
O Allah, place light in my heart, light in my sight, light in my hearing...

Upon Entering The Mosque

اللَّهمَّ افْتَحْ لِي أَبْوَابَ رَحْمَتِكَ
Allahummaa- aftah li abwaba rahmatik
O Allah! Open for me the gates of Your mercy.

Upon Exiting The Mosque

اللَّهمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ مِنْ فَضْلِكَ
Allahumma-inee ass aluka min fadlik
O Allah! I beg of You of Your Grace.

Upon Hearing The Adhan

اللَّهُمَّ رَبَّ هَذِهِ الدَّعْوَةِ التَّامَّةِ...
Allahumma rabba hadihi-d-da wati-t-tammati...
O Allah, Lord of this perfect call and established prayer...

Before Entering the Restroom

بِسْمِ اللهِ اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْخُبُْثِ وَالْخَبَائِثِ
[Bismillah] Allahuma innee a’oothu bika minal khubthi wal Khabaa’ith
In the name of Allah, O Allah, I seek refuge in You from unclean spirits, [male and female devils].

After Leaving the Restroom

غُفْرَانَكَ
Ghufraanak
Grant me Forgiveness.

Upon Entering the House

اللَّهمّ اني اسألك خير المولج وخير المخرج بِسْمِ اللَّهِ وَلَجْنَا، وَبِسْمِ اللَّهِ خَرَجْنَا، وَعَلَى رَبِّنَا تَوَكَّلْنَا
Alahumma inni assaluka kheryil mawlij wakheryil makh’ridge, Bismillah walijna, wa Bismillah kharajna, wa alalah rabana tawakalna
O Allah! I ask you for good both when entering and when going out. In the name of Allah we have entered, and in the name of Allah we have gone out, and in our Lord we count.

Upon Getting Angry

أَعُوْذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيْمِ
Aootho bilahee mina shaytaan irajeem
I seek refuge with Allah from Satan, the accursed.
