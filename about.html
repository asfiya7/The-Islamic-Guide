10 Important Things in Islam

1. Shahada (Declaration of Faith)

The Shahada is the testimony of faith: "There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is the messenger of Allah."

2. Five Pillars of Islam

The Five Pillars are the core acts of worship: Shahada, Salah, Zakat, Sawm, and Hajj.

3. Tawhid (Oneness of Allah)

Tawhid refers to the belief in the oneness and uniqueness of Allah, without any partners.

4. Prophethood (Nubuwwah)

Muslims believe in the prophets sent by Allah, with the final prophet being Muhammad (PBUH).

5. Qur'an (The Holy Book)

The Qur'an is the literal word of Allah, revealed to Prophet Muhammad, and is the primary source of guidance for Muslims.

6. Angels (Mala'ika)

Muslims believe in angels who have various roles, including delivering revelations and recording deeds.

7. Day of Judgment (Yawm al-Qiyamah)

On the Day of Judgment, all individuals will be resurrected and judged by Allah for their deeds.

8. Islamic Ethics and Morality

Islam emphasizes high moral standards, such as honesty, kindness, and generosity in daily life.

9. Jihad (Struggle or Striving)

Jihad refers to the spiritual, moral, and physical struggle in the way of Allah, including striving for justice.

10. Respect for Parents and Elders

Islam stresses the importance of showing kindness and respect to parents and elders.
